# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information (physical address, email, etc.) without explicit permission
- Conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers through GitHub's private vulnerability reporting form (see `SECURITY.md`) or by opening a private security advisory on this repository.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.
